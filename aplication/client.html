33, 45, 49. Pertamina RU VI Balongan:

Plant Air Compressors Panel Retrofit for Reliability of Utilities

In 2012-2013 and 2015-2016, PT. Pertamina RU VI Balongan appointed us do a project of Compressor Panel Retrofit for Reliability of Utilities. In the project, we had to manage controller and instruments change of the existing air compressors to enable communication with the Central control room. Also to rectify reliability of the Plant Air Compressor (58-K-101A/B/C/.D) which serve the supply of compressed air to all areas of the refinery process operation area and supply for the process of Instrumentation (pneumatic) since it first operated in year 1994. The Scope was PLC-ASC Plant Air Compressor, Transmitters within Racks, Installation, startup, and commissioning.

39. 39. TAC Pertamina EP – BWP Meruap:

Gas production facility at lapangan Sarolangun

In 2014, we supplied 4 units of Natural Gas Screw Compressor to PT.Petrotech Pentanusa, a subsidiary of Rukun Raharja Group, and the awarded contractor for Rental Service of Gas Production Facility at Lapangan Meruap, Sarolangun – Jambi. Within this facility, they deliver gas explored by TAC Pertamina Meruap to local state owned gas power plant (5 MW) Serumpun Pseko. Local to expand electrification coverage at the region.

40. PLN Enjiniring:

CNG Plant Bangkanai, Central Kalimantan

In 2015, we supported PT. Timas Suplindo in cooperation with PT Odira Energi as consortium to run a project that was owned by PT. PLN Enjiniring. The project was to construct CNG Plant Bangkanai Plant, a facility that supply and process (cleaning, conditioning and gas storaging) CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) from OPHIR Gas Station to PLTMG (Pembangkit Listrik Tenaga Mesin Gas) 155 MW Bangkanai located at Karendan Village, Lahei District, North Barito Regency, Central Kalimantan.

This project was not only successfully upgraded PLTMG capacity of electricity, but also improve efficiency as CNG replacing diesel fuel.
